Partners & Collaboration

We welcome partners who share our vision for inclusive digital opportunity across Malawi and Africa.

Collaborate With Us

Together we expand tech education and digital inclusion

Partnerships help us reach more young people, schools, and communities with programmes that matter. HealingTech Initiative operates independently and is sustained through grants, donations, partnerships, and community support.

Schools & Universities

Bring digital skills training, AI literacy workshops, mentorship, and inclusion programmes to your students. We co-design curriculum-aligned sessions for primary, secondary, and tertiary institutions.

NGOs & Community Groups

Partner with us to deliver supportive guidance, digital inclusion, and youth empowerment in underserved communities — combining grassroots reach with Labs-powered innovation.

Government & Public Sector

Collaborate on digital inclusion policy, youth employment initiatives, accessibility programmes, and community technology projects aligned with national development goals.

Companies & Corporates

Support our mission through sponsorships, equipment donations, pro-bono expertise, employee volunteer programmes, and CSR partnerships that create measurable social impact.

International Organisations

Work with us on cross-border programmes, funding partnerships, research collaborations, and scalable models for digital inclusion across Africa.

Tech & Innovation Partners

Collaborate on software, platforms, and technology that strengthens our community programmes. Our technology partner, HealingTech Labs, was born from our Initiative to create employment and product-building opportunities for the youth we train — while operating independently.

Why Partner With HealingTech Initiative?

When you partner with us, you join a technology-driven social impact organization working for inclusive digital opportunity across Malawi, Kenya, and beyond. Together we deliver education, inclusion, and empowerment programmes that create lasting community impact.
