About HealingTech Initiative

A youth-led non-profit organisation using technology to create lasting impact — through digital skills, mentorship, and psychosocial support for underserved communities across Malawi and Kenya.

Our Commitment

Technology as a Catalyst for Inclusive Development

HealingTech Initiative exists to ensure that technology creates opportunity for everyone — by investing in people and communities across Malawi and Africa.

We believe every young person deserves access to digital opportunities regardless of financial status, disability, gender, or location. We are a youth-led non-profit organisation sustained through grants, donations, partnerships, and community support.

Our Focus Areas

Technology Partner

HealingTech Labs was born out of our Initiative to create employment and earning opportunities for the youth we train — while operating as an independent technology partner.

Investing in People

From digital skills training to scholarships and mentorship, we put human potential at the centre of everything we do.

Vision for Africa

Starting in Malawi and Kenya, we are building toward inclusive digital opportunity across the continent.

Technology Partner: HealingTech Labs

HealingTech Labs was born out of HealingTech Initiative — extending our mission from training into real opportunity. Labs creates employment pathways for the youth we train, helping them earn a living, grow their skills, and in some cases develop products of their own. HealingTech Labs operates independently, while collaborating with HealingTech Initiative through technology expertise and strategic support. HealingTech Initiative remains a youth-led non-profit sustained through grants, donations, partnerships, and community contributions.
