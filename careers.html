Careers at The HealingTech Initiative

Build the future of technology in Malawi. Join a mission-driven team engineering world-class software and social impact.

Join Us

Why Work at The HealingTech Initiative?

At The HealingTech Initiative, you'll work on meaningful projects that shape Malawi's digital landscape — from AI platforms and enterprise software to government systems and e-commerce. We're a growing team that values craftsmanship, continuous learning, and technology that gives back.

Every project you build contributes to the HealingTech Initiative, funding digital skills training and youth empowerment across Africa.

Open Roles

Current Opportunities

We accept open applications for the roles below. Specific openings will be posted as they become available.

Open Application

Software Engineer

Build scalable web applications, APIs, and AI-powered features using React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, and Python. Experience with cloud deployment and PostgreSQL is a plus.

What you'll do: Design and implement full-stack features, write clean maintainable code, collaborate on architecture decisions, and contribute to Malawi's digital transformation.

Open Application

UI/UX Designer

Create intuitive, accessible interfaces for web and mobile products. Strong portfolio in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design required.

What you'll do: Lead design from concept to delivery, conduct user testing, maintain design systems, and ensure every product meets global UX standards.

Open Application

Project Manager

Coordinate software projects from discovery to delivery. Experience in agile methodologies, client communication, and technology project management preferred.

What you'll do: Manage timelines and deliverables, facilitate client communication, coordinate cross-functional teams, and ensure projects launch on time and on budget.

Open Application

Intern

Hands-on learning opportunity for students and recent graduates passionate about technology. Rotations across engineering, design, and project management.

What you'll do: Learn from experienced mentors, contribute to real projects, build your portfolio, and gain exposure to enterprise software development in Malawi.

How to Apply

Send your CV, portfolio (if applicable), and a brief cover letter to:

info@healingtechinitiative.org

Include the role title in your subject line. We review all applications and will contact shortlisted candidates.
