Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about The HealingTech Initiative, our services, and the HealingTech Initiative.

About The HealingTech Initiative

What is The HealingTech Initiative?
The HealingTech Initiative is a youth-led non-profit organisation that empowers young people through technology, mentorship, and psychosocial support across Malawi and Kenya — with a focus on digital skills, mental wellness, and community impact.
What services does The HealingTech Initiative offer?
We offer AI and machine learning, software engineering, web and mobile development, cloud infrastructure, enterprise software, government digital systems, e-commerce platforms, learning platforms, business automation, API development, cybersecurity-conscious development, and digital transformation consulting.
Who does The HealingTech Initiative work with?
We partner with businesses, government agencies, NGOs, educational institutions, and startups across Malawi and the wider region. Our solutions are tailored to organisations of all sizes seeking to modernise their technology.
What technologies does The HealingTech Initiative use?
Our core stack includes React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, Python, AI/OpenAI integrations, PostgreSQL, Docker, cloud platforms, and REST APIs. We select technologies based on project requirements, scalability needs, and long-term maintainability.
How do I start a project with The HealingTech Initiative?
Reach out via our Contact page or email info@healingtechinitiative.org. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your goals, scope, and timeline, then propose a tailored technology roadmap.

About the HealingTech Initiative

What is the HealingTech Initiative?
The HealingTech Initiative focuses on digital skills training, scholarships, technology education, digital inclusion, women in technology, youth empowerment, and community innovation programmes across Africa.
How does The HealingTech Initiative create impact?
The HealingTech Initiative is a youth-led non-profit organisation that combines digital skills training, mentorship, psychosocial support, and community programmes to empower young people across Malawi and Kenya. Learn more on our Programs page.
Can I volunteer or partner with the Initiative?
Yes. We welcome volunteers, corporate partners, and donors who share our vision for youth empowerment through technology. Visit our Volunteer page, Partners page, or Donate page to get involved.
Where does the Initiative operate?
The HealingTech Initiative has active projects in Malawi (including the Clear Sight Initiative in Lilongwe) and Kenya (Mathari Mentorship Project in Nairobi), with plans to expand across Africa.

General

Does The HealingTech Initiative hire?
We welcome open applications for Software Engineer, UI/UX Designer, Project Manager, and Intern roles. Send your CV and cover letter to info@healingtechinitiative.org. See our Careers page for details.
Where is The HealingTech Initiative located?
The HealingTech Initiative is headquartered in Lilongwe, Malawi, with operations and Initiative projects extending to Nairobi, Kenya, and across the region.
