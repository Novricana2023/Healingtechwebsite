Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about HealingTech Initiative — our mission, programmes, and how we empower communities through technology.

About HealingTech Initiative

What is HealingTech Initiative?
HealingTech Initiative is a technology-driven social impact organization combining technology, AI, education, digital skills, innovation, inclusive support, healthcare access, community empowerment, and social innovation — with counselling and mental health support for people navigating difficult circumstances.
What services does HealingTech Initiative offer?
We offer AI and machine learning, software engineering, web and mobile development, cloud infrastructure, enterprise software, government digital systems, e-commerce platforms, learning platforms, business automation, API development, cybersecurity-conscious development, and digital transformation consulting.
Who does HealingTech Initiative work with?
We partner with businesses, government agencies, NGOs, educational institutions, and startups across Malawi and the wider region. Our solutions are tailored to organisations of all sizes seeking to modernise their technology.
What technologies does HealingTech Initiative use?
Our core stack includes React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, Python, AI/OpenAI integrations, PostgreSQL, Docker, cloud platforms, and REST APIs. We select technologies based on project requirements, scalability needs, and long-term maintainability.
How do I start a project with HealingTech Initiative?
Reach out via our Contact page or email info@healingtechinitiative.org. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your goals, scope, and timeline, then propose a tailored technology roadmap.

Our Programmes & Impact

What programmes does HealingTech Initiative run?
HealingTech Initiative focuses on digital skills training, scholarships, technology education, digital inclusion, women in technology, youth innovation, and community programmes — advancing inclusion and opportunity across Africa.
How does HealingTech Initiative create impact?
HealingTech Initiative combines digital skills training, mentorship, supportive guidance, and community programmes to empower young people across Malawi and Kenya — facilitating resilience and recovery while building pathways to success. Learn more on our Initiatives page.
Can I volunteer or partner with the Initiative?
Yes. We welcome volunteers, corporate partners, and donors who share our vision for youth empowerment through technology. Visit our Volunteer page, Partners page, or Donate page to get involved.
Where does the Initiative operate?
HealingTech Initiative has active projects in Malawi (including the Clear Sight Initiative in Lilongwe) and Kenya (Mathari Mentorship Project in Nairobi), with plans to expand across Africa.

General

Does HealingTech Initiative hire?
We welcome open applications for Software Engineer, UI/UX Designer, Project Manager, and Intern roles. Send your CV and cover letter to info@healingtechinitiative.org. See our Careers page for details.
Where is HealingTech Initiative located?
HealingTech Initiative is headquartered in Lilongwe, Malawi, with operations and Initiative projects extending to Nairobi, Kenya, and across the region.
