Our Focus Areas

Using technology as a catalyst for inclusive development — ensuring every young person has access to digital opportunity, regardless of financial status, disability, gender, or location.

Digital Skills Training

Purpose: Equip youth with coding, computer literacy, and career-ready digital skills.

Impact: Bridging the digital divide in underserved communities across Malawi and Kenya.

Vision: A generation of digitally confident young leaders across Africa.

Scholarships & Educational Support

Purpose: Remove financial barriers to quality technology education.

Impact: Enabling talented youth to pursue learning regardless of economic background.

Vision: Sustainable scholarship pipelines that expand access to technology education.

Technology for Persons with Disabilities

Purpose: Deliver assistive technology and inclusive digital access.

Impact: Projects like Clear Sight Initiative for children with albinism.

Vision: Technology that adapts to every ability.

AI & Digital Literacy

Purpose: Introduce AI concepts and responsible digital citizenship to youth.

Impact: Preparing the next generation for an AI-powered future.

Vision: Malawi as a leader in inclusive AI education.

Women & Girls in Technology

Purpose: Close the gender gap in STEM and technology careers.

Impact: Fellowships, mentorship, and safe learning spaces for girls.

Vision: Equal representation of women in Africa's tech ecosystem.

Youth Innovation

Purpose: Foster creative problem-solving and entrepreneurial thinking.

Impact: Innovation challenges that turn ideas into community solutions.

Vision: Youth-led innovation hubs across the continent.

Community Digital Transformation

Purpose: Bring digital tools and connectivity to local communities.

Impact: Community tech labs and digital access programmes.

Vision: Every community connected and digitally empowered.

Entrepreneurship

Purpose: Support youth to build tech-enabled businesses and social enterprises.

Impact: Vocational training and startup mentorship.

Vision: A thriving youth entrepreneurship ecosystem in Malawi.

Research & Innovation

Purpose: Generate evidence and insights on technology for social impact.

Impact: Data-driven programmes and best practices.

Vision: Research that shapes policy and practice across Africa.

Career Development

Purpose: Connect youth to internships, jobs, and professional pathways.

Impact: Career guidance integrated with digital skills training.

Vision: Clear pathways from learning to meaningful employment.

Mentorship & Counselling

Purpose: Pair youth with mentors and provide counselling support for people in difficult situations.

Impact: Mathari Mentorship Project and ongoing mentor networks across Malawi and Kenya.

Vision: Every young person supported with hope, guidance, and pathways to recovery.
