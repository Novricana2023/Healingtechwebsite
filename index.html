At HealingTech Initiative, we are a youth-led non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering children and youth who have experienced trauma, have disabilities, or are economically challenged, by equipping them with the tools, skills, and support they need to thrive. Our approach focuses on healing through technology, mental health, and emotional well-being, helping young people build resilience, foster self-esteem, and prepare for a brighter future. We believe that every child has the potential to succeed, regardless of their background. That’s why we focus on creating a safe and supportive space where youth can learn, grow, and discover their strengths. Through our innovative programs, we provide digital skills training, mentorship, and emotional intelligence support to help them overcome challenges and become leaders in their communities.
To provide young people, particularly those traumatized or from marginalized groups, with digital skills training, mentorship, and psychosocial support to enhance their personal growth and career advancement.
To create a world where technology serves as a bridge to healing, empowerment, and sustainable opportunities for youth and children in adversity.

Who We Are

A Social Initiative Powered by Technology

HealingTech Initiative is a youth-led non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering children and youth who have experienced trauma, have disabilities, or are economically challenged. We provide digital skills training, mentorship, and psychosocial support — helping young people build resilience and access opportunity across Malawi, Kenya, and beyond.

Who We Are

Why We Exist

Every young person deserves access to digital opportunities regardless of financial status, disability, gender, or location. Technology must create opportunity for everyone.

Who We Serve

Children and youth who have experienced trauma, have disabilities, or are economically challenged — across underserved communities in Malawi, Kenya, and beyond.

Technology Partner: HealingTech Labs

HealingTech Labs was born out of HealingTech Initiative — extending our mission from training into real opportunity. Labs creates employment pathways for the youth we train, helping them earn a living, grow their skills, and in some cases develop products of their own. HealingTech Labs operates independently, while collaborating with HealingTech Initiative through technology expertise and strategic support. HealingTech Initiative remains a youth-led non-profit sustained through grants, donations, partnerships, and community contributions.
