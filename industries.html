Industries We Serve

Deep sector expertise across Malawi's key industries — delivering technology that understands local context and global standards.

Healthcare

Patient management systems, telehealth platforms, health data analytics, and clinic workflow tools that improve care delivery and operational efficiency across Malawi's health sector.

Education

Learning management systems, student portals, digital assessment tools, and education administration platforms for schools, universities, and training institutions.

Government

E-governance portals, citizen service platforms, digital identity workflows, and secure internal systems that modernise public administration and improve transparency.

NGOs

Programme management tools, beneficiary tracking, donor reporting dashboards, and field data collection systems for non-profits operating across Malawi and the region.

SMEs

Affordable business software, e-commerce storefronts, CRM systems, and automation tools that help small and medium enterprises compete and grow in the digital economy.

Financial Services

Secure fintech integrations, loan management platforms, payment processing systems, and compliance-ready applications for banks, MFIs, and financial institutions.

Retail

Point-of-sale integrations, inventory management, omnichannel e-commerce, and customer loyalty platforms for retailers serving urban and rural markets.

Logistics

Fleet tracking, route optimisation, warehouse management, and supply chain visibility tools that streamline distribution across Malawi's transport networks.

Agriculture

AgriTech platforms for cooperatives and exporters — crop monitoring, market linkage systems, farmer registries, and data-driven advisory tools.

Manufacturing

Production tracking, quality management, ERP modules, and IoT-ready dashboards that help manufacturers optimise operations and meet export standards.
