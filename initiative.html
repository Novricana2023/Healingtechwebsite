HealingTech Initiative

Creating a world where technology serves as a bridge to healing, empowerment, and sustainable opportunities for youth and children in adversity.

Our Model

Where Technology Meets Compassionate Care

At HealingTech Initiative, we unite technology, AI, education, digital skills, innovation, inclusive support, healthcare access, and community empowerment — alongside counselling and mental health support for people in difficult situations — to help youth facing adversity heal, recover, and thrive across Malawi, Kenya, and Africa.
