Our Programs

To leverage technology to improve lives — through education, digital skills, innovation, inclusive support, healthcare access, and community empowerment for youth and children across Africa.

Current Work

Ongoing Programmes

Programmes we are actively running now — building digital skills and inclusion with communities across Malawi and Kenya.

Ongoing

Future Coders Program

An introductory-to-intermediate coding pathway for youth aged 12–22, designed to build confidence in programming, problem-solving, and creative technology.

Ongoing

Digital Inclusion Program

Bridging access gaps by bringing devices, connectivity support, and foundational digital literacy to underserved communities across rural and urban Malawi.

Our Track Record

Previous Projects

Community initiatives we have delivered — creating lasting impact in Lilongwe and Nairobi.

Previous Project · Malawi

Clear Sight Initiative

Through the Clear Sight Initiative, we provided optical glasses to children with albinism in Lilongwe, improving their vision and enhancing their learning experience. We also introduced digital skills training to empower them with knowledge and opportunities for the future.

Lilongwe, Malawi  ·  December 2024 – January 2025

Previous Project · Kenya

Mathari Mentorship & Digital Skills Project

At Mathari, a school in the slums of Nairobi, we delivered mentorship and digital skills training — guidance on wellbeing, emotional intelligence, and career development alongside essential tech skills for underserved youth.

Nairobi, Kenya  ·  2024
