Resources

What we build and share with underserved communities — education materials, capacity building, digital skills, and practical tools for lasting growth.

What We Provide

Building capability, not dependency

HealingTech Initiative equips young people and communities with the knowledge, skills, and support structures they need to thrive — especially in areas where access to technology and quality education is limited.

Education Materials

Curriculum guides, learning kits, and accessible digital content designed for youth in underserved schools and community settings across Malawi and Kenya.

Capacity Building

Workshops and training for educators, community leaders, and local partners — strengthening the systems that deliver education and psychosocial support on the ground.

Digital Skills

Coding, computer literacy, AI awareness, and career-ready technology training — opening pathways for youth who would otherwise be left behind.

Mentorship & Guidance

Structured mentorship, emotional intelligence support, and career guidance — helping young people build confidence and navigate their futures.

Inclusion & Accessibility

Assistive tools, inclusive learning approaches, and programmes for children with disabilities — including projects like Clear Sight for children with albinism.

Community Programmes

On-the-ground initiatives in Lilongwe, Nairobi, and surrounding communities — combining technology, mentorship, and local partnership for real outcomes.
