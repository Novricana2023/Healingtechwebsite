HealingTech supports the following SDGs

SDG 8: Decent Work & Economic Growth

Vocational and entrepreneurial training to support sustainability, food security, and youth employment.

Empowering young leaders to drive innovation, advocate for inclusion, and create lasting community impact.

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-being

Promoting wellbeing awareness and supportive mentorship for youth facing adversity — facilitating resilience and recovery.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Digital and technological skills training plus educational support for underserved youth.

SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

Building partnerships with stakeholders to deliver sustainable, scalable results.

SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities

Supporting youth facing social and economic barriers including children with albinism and economically vulnerable communities.
