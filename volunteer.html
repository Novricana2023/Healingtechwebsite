Become a Volunteer

Join HealingTech Initiative and help empower communities through technology — building pathways to resilience and success for young people across Africa.

Volunteer Application Form

Join Our Team

Your skills can change young lives

HealingTech Initiative is a technology-driven social impact organization committed to inclusive digital opportunity. We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds who want to make a real difference.

Who we are looking for:

  • Professionals — share your expertise in tech, business, or social impact
  • Students — gain hands-on experience in tech-for-good projects
  • Educators — help design and deliver learning programmes
  • Designers & developers — build accessible tools and learning experiences
  • Mentors — guide youth through skills development and career pathways

Fill in the application form and our team will review your submission and get back to you. Flexible remote and in-person opportunities are available across Malawi, Kenya, and beyond.
