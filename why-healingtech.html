Why HealingTech Matters

Measurable Challenges. Purposeful Response.

Across Malawi and much of Africa, children and youth face measurable barriers to education, digital opportunity, wellbeing, and inclusion. HealingTech exists to respond to evidence — not assumptions — with technology, skills, and compassionate support.

Focus: Malawi — with lessons and partnerships across Kenya and the wider African continent.

HealingTech responds through education support, digital skills training, and programmes that re-engage vulnerable youth.

13.5% rural · 40.7% urban

internet access gap — rural vs urban

Rural communities face dramatically lower connectivity — widening gaps in education, healthcare information, and economic participation.

HealingTech responds by prioritising rural and underserved communities in digital inclusion and community tech initiatives.

Source: Malawi NSO / Freedom House (2023–2024)
0%

usage gap — covered but not connected

Network coverage exists, but affordability, devices, and digital literacy keep millions from using mobile internet.

HealingTech responds with device access, foundational digital literacy, and hands-on skills that turn coverage into opportunity.

Source: UNDP Digital Readiness Assessment (2025)
0%

youth unemployment

With a young, fast-growing population and a largely informal labour market, too many youth lack pathways to stable work.

HealingTech responds by building employable tech skills and connecting trained youth to opportunities through HealingTech Labs.

Source: World Bank Malawi (2023)
950,000

children & adolescents out of school

Hundreds of thousands of children remain out of school — girls and southern/central region districts are disproportionately affected.

HealingTech responds with inclusive programmes for girls, vulnerable youth, and communities facing the steepest barriers.

Source: World Bank OOSCA Estimate (2020)
1 in 5

youth face exclusion from opportunity

Children with disabilities, girls, and youth in financial hardship are often last to benefit from digital and educational advances.

HealingTech responds through Clear Sight–style inclusion, assistive support, and technology designed for accessibility.

Source: World Bank / UNICEF-aligned estimates (2020–2024)
